WELCOME TO BTECH!

Business technology early college high school is a 9th-14th grade school. Our school was opened in September 2014 in partnership with SAP & Queensborough community college. Any student attending business technology early college high school will learn how to let the world run using technology to solve everyday word problems. Our students have up to 6 years to earn a high school diploma & in addition to an associates degree for free of cost from Queensborough community college.

SAP

In our school the schedule switches up a little. Depending on if you take qcc classes some days kids are off the btech campus & are at qcc. But the kids on btech campus start 8:40 everyday & end 3:30 everyday except Wednesdays they end at 2:10.

Students also get a hands-on job with SAP. Our students will become expert readers, writers, & critical thinkers with the technical skill to be succeed on modern information. Our students will have the knowledge, skills, career, & technical educations certifications, & work experience that will make the graduates much more competitive for employment.
